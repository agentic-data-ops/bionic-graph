This document is used to build a graph to simulate social activities.

## Social Activities

### 1. Hotpot Gatherings (Monthly Tradition)

**"Chengdu Youth Circle Monthly Hotpot"** — Every first Saturday of the month, the group gathers for Sichuan hotpot at their favorite restaurant near Chunxi Road.

**Recent event — June 2026**:
- **Venue**: Da Miao Hotpot (大妙火锅), Chunxi Road
- **Attendees**: All 10 members (Zhao Lei was 30 min late due to a startup investor call)
- **Activities**: Eating mala (spicy) broth, drinking beer, Wang Qiang told stories about his crazy client meetings in Chongqing, Lin Xiao showed everyone her new spring collection photos, Zhou Ming's son Xiao Jun came along and played with Zhang Wei's daughter Xiao Mei
- **Highlight**: Zhao Lei announced Sun Fang is pregnant with a girl — everyone cheered and ordered a second round of beer
- **Bill**: 886 RMB — split evenly (Wang Qiang paid for Zhao Lei since he just bought a house)

**Previous event — May 2026**:
- **Venue**: Huang Cheng Laoma (皇城老妈), Qin Tai Road
- **Attendees**: 9 (Zhou Ming was absent — his son had a fever)
- **Activities**: Celebrated Chen Jie's 29th birthday with a cake surprise organized by Liu Yang. Wang Qiang brought two bottles of Maotai (gifts from a client). Ended at 11 PM.
- **Bill**: Zhao Lei covered it as a celebration for his startup's Series A funding

- **Route**: Starting from Jincheng Lake → along the Greenway → stop at San Sheng Hua Xiang for photos → return
- **Breakfast stop**: Soy milk and youtiao (fried dough sticks) at a small shop in San Sheng Hua Xiang
- **Post-ride**: Huang Yu treated everyone to cold brew coffee at a new café

**Planned ride — Qingcheng Mountain (青城山)**:
